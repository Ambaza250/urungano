Amakuru yizewe kandi yoroshye kumenya

Ese ni iki nakora ngo ngabanye uburibwe mu gihe cy'imihango?

Kubabara mu nda mu gihe cy’imihango ni ibintu bisanzwe. Ariko iyo ububabare butihanganirwa, ihutire kuya kwa muganga.

Ibintu byagufasha:

  • Gukoresha amazi ashyushye ku nda cyangwa koga amazi ashyushye
  • Gukora imyitozo ngororamubiri yoroheje (kugendagenda, yoga)
  • Kurya indyo yuzuye no kunywa amazi menshi
  • Kuruhuka no guhumeka byimbitse
  • Gukanda ahantu habugenewe buhoro buhoro

Ese ni ibihe bimenyetso bishobora kunyereka ko ngiye kujya mu mihango?

  • Kubabara umutwe, kubabara mu kiziba cy’inda
  • Kubabara no kubyimba amabere
  • Gutura umubi, kugira umunaniro
  • Kumva udashaka kurya cyangwa ushaka kurya cyane
  • Kumva ubabaye cyangwa udatuje

VIH / SIDA - Kwirinda no Kwirindwa

VIH ni iki?

VIH ni virusi yibasira uturemangingo turinda umubiri. Iyo itavuwe ishobora gutera SIDA.

Uko yanduramo:

  • Gukora imibonano mpuzabitsina idakingiye
  • Gusangira ibikoresho by'amaraso byanduye
  • Kwanduzwa n’umubyeyi ku mwana

Uko wirinda VIH:

  • Gukoresha agakingirizo buri gihe
  • Kugira umukunzi umwe gusa
  • Kwipimisha VIH
  • Gukoresha PrEP na PEP

Ubutumwa bw'ingenzi:

Kwipimisha ni byiza. Niba ufite ubwoba cyangwa ibibazo, hamagara 3530 cyangwa uje kwa muganga hakiri kare.
