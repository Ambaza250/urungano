About Zamuka Youth Platform

Empowering Rwandan youth to build financial independence through digital savings, trust-based credit, and practical education.

Our Mission

To empower Youth including University of Rwanda students with low and unstable incomes by providing a secure Digital Peer-to-Peer ecosystem that integrates micro-savings, collateral-free capital, and practical financial education.

We aim to break the financial barriers for young women, marginalized youth and other youth in general, transforming them from job seekers into sustainable job creators.

Our Vision

To be the leading accelerator for financial independence among African youth, fostering a generation of "Investment-Ready" innovators who drive Rwanda’s Vision 2050 through inclusive growth, digital trust, and private sector excellence.

About Us

Zamuka youth platform is a digital saving and credit platform designed for Rwandan youth with low and unstable income. The platform allows users to save small amounts starting from 500 RWF per week, build financial discipline, and access affordable loans based on their saving behavior.

The platform combines saving, borrowing, and financial education to help youth become financially independent and support small business creation.
